FINAL POEM

I entered searching

for another high score.


Neon lights blinked,

coins echoed,

and every level

asked me to continue.


Every enemy

was only another word.

Every puzzle

became another verse.


I learned

that victory

is not measured

by points alone,

but by every story

we choose to finish.


The arcade sleeps.

The screen flickers.

The controller rests.


Yet somewhere

a single pixel

still glows.


Waiting…

for another player.
